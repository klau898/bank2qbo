FAQ

How is this different from DocuClipper / Veryfi / Dext?

Those are full-auto OCR — fast, but you have to audit every row anyway. We add human verification + a per-bank library + a 99% reconciliation guarantee, so you can hand the output straight to a client without re-checking. For one-off cleanup jobs the $39 flat fee usually beats the $34/mo Dext subscription.

Can I really upload a 200-page PDF?

Yes. Single tier covers up to 100 pages; for larger, drop us a line at support@bank2qbo.com and we'll quote per-page above 100.

My client has a weird bank — will it work?

If it's a US bank (state, regional, credit union, fintech like Mercury / Bluevine / Chime) and the PDF has selectable text, yes — we add new bank formats weekly. If we can't get above 99% reconciliation, full refund.

What about scanned PDFs (photographed or image-only)?

Scanned bank statements (no selectable text — pure image scans) are handled but with extra manual review and a slower turnaround (24-48h instead of 4-24h). Reconciliation guarantee still applies. If your statement is image-only, please flag it in your email so I can prioritize manual extraction.
