# Bank2QBO — Direct Message Scripts (Human-Sent by Klau)

**IMPORTANT:** Per FL TCPA + federal CAN-SPAM, AI-generated mass outreach
without consent is a legal liability. These scripts are designed for **Klau
to send personally, one at a time**, to people in his existing professional
network (LinkedIn connections, IG follows, WhatsApp warm contacts).

Per Codex peer review:
> "Send no more than 25/day. Min account quality: 500+ karma/profile age,
> conversational history, no link-spam. Frame as ASK ABOUT THEIR WORKFLOW,
> not as PITCH."

---

## Target lists (Klau pulls 25-30 from existing network)

### LinkedIn — search filters
- "Bookkeeper" + "Miami" or "Florida" (≤500 employees company size)
- "QuickBooks ProAdvisor" (anywhere US, 1st-degree connections preferred)
- "Solo accountant" / "Independent CPA"
- Klau's own LinkedIn 1st-degree connections in accounting/finance

### Instagram — accounts to DM
- Bookkeepers who post on accounting hashtags (#bookkeepertips, #cleanbooks, #quickbookspro)

- Anyone in Klau's WhatsApp address book who runs a small business + does their own books

**Subject (if applicable):** Quick bookkeeper question

>
> Random Friday question: when a client sends you a PDF bank statement for a
> month they forgot to give you on time, what's your current workflow? Retype it
> manually, run it through some converter, ask a junior to do it?
>
> Reason I ask — I'm building something quietly and want to make sure I understand
> the actual pain before I assume. No pitch yet, just curious how you do it today.
>
> — Klau (Cordoba) / Munda LLC

- For someone who posts about QBO frustrations: lead with "Saw your post about [topic] last week"
- For Hispanic-network folks: bilingual — open in Spanish, switch if they reply in English

## Anti-pattern — what NOT to send

❌ Mass-send identical script with `{name}` placeholder visible — caught immediately
❌ Pitch in the first DM — kills the reply rate to 0
❌ Lead with "AI-powered" or "revolutionary" — accountant audience hates AI marketing language
❌ Send a link before they ask — IG flags this, LinkedIn dampens reach
❌ Send 50 in one hour — platform spam-filters trigger

---

## Pacing

- **5-10 DMs/day max**, spread across morning + afternoon
- **Different scripts** per platform (LinkedIn formal, IG casual, WhatsApp Spanish)
- **Personalize the first sentence** for each (Klau reads their recent post/profile)
- **Log every send + every reply** in concierge.db `leads` table
- **Reply within 2 hours** during business hours — the warm thread is the asset
